▲ Bullish Reversal
HAMMER
BULLISH REVERSAL
  • Small body near the TOP
  • Long lower wick = 2–3× the body
  • Little to no upper wick
  • MUST occur after a downtrend
⚠️ KEY: Size doesn't make a hammer — structure + location does. Large body = NOT a hammer (your 13:45 lesson).
INVERTED HAMMER
BULLISH REVERSAL
  • Small body at the BOTTOM
  • Long upper wick = 2–3× the body
  • Little to no lower wick
  • Appears after downtrend
Signal: Bulls attempted to push higher. Needs confirmation next candle — weaker than hammer alone.
BULLISH ENGULFING
BULLISH REVERSAL
  • Large green candle fully covers prior red
  • Green body opens below prior close
  • Green body closes above prior open
  • Appears after downtrend
Signal: Strong reversal — buyers overwhelmed sellers completely. Higher volume = stronger signal.
MORNING STAR
BULLISH REVERSAL

  • Candle 2: Small doji/star — gap down
  • Candle 3: Large green closing into red body
Signal: High-probability reversal. Most reliable at key support levels with volume confirmation.

  • Open and close are virtually equal

  • Appears in any trend

SPINNING TOP
INDECISION
  • Small real body (any color)
  • Long wicks on BOTH sides
  • Body smaller than wicks
  • Common in choppy markets
LTPro Rule: MID-RANGE spinning tops = stay out. This is your "sit on your hands" candle.

🚨 THE GOLDEN RULE

A candle pattern WITHOUT context = meaningless. Always ask: (1) Where is it? (2) What happened before it? (3) Is it at support/resistance? (4) Is volume confirming? — Structure + location > size.

🧠 THE UPGRADE — Think Like This

Stop asking "What candle is this?" — Start asking "What is this candle telling me given WHERE it is?" Same shape = completely different meaning depending on location.

📍 LOCATION RULES

01Hammer at support = High prob bullish reversal ✅
02Hammer in mid-range = Meaningless ❌
03Bearish Engulfing at resistance = Strong short signal ✅
04Doji after strong trend = Reversal warning ⚠️
05Doji in choppy range = Noise, ignore ❌
06Marubozu at breakout = Ride momentum ✅

📊 TREND CONTEXT

01Big red in downtrend = Continuation, NOT reversal
02Big red after breakout up = Pullback, watch bounce
03Spinning tops in uptrend = Digestion, likely continues
04Spinning tops at resistance = Bulls struggling
05Higher lows = Bullish structure regardless of candle color
06Lower highs = Bearish structure — wait for break
