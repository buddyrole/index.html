▲ Bullish Reversal
HAMMER
BULLISH REVERSAL
  • Small body near the TOP
  • Lower wick ≥ 2× the body
  • Little to no upper wick
  • MUST occur after a downtrend
📖 Story: Sellers pushed price down hard — but buyers stepped in and REJECTED those lower prices. Bulls won the battle.
❌ Failure: If next candle breaks below hammer low → pattern failed → becomes continuation down. Exit.
⚠️ KEY: Large body = NOT a hammer. Wick ≥ 2× body is non-negotiable. Your 13:45 lesson.
BULLISH ENGULFING
BULLISH REVERSAL
  • Large green candle fully covers prior red
  • Opens below prior close, closes above prior open
  • Appears after downtrend
📖 Story: Sellers were in control — then buyers came in with such force they completely overpowered the prior session. One side got overwhelmed.
❌ Failure: Price returns back inside the red candle body → squeeze setup resolving bearish. Watch for puts.
MORNING STAR
BULLISH REVERSAL
  • 3-candle: Red → small star → Green
  • Star shows sellers losing momentum
  • Green closes deep into red body
📖 Story: Sellers dominated (red). Then — indecision. Then buyers took over completely. Three acts of a reversal story playing out candle by candle.
❌ Failure: Green candle fails to close above 50% of red body → incomplete reversal. Wait for more confirmation.
DRAGONFLY DOJI
BULLISH LEAN
  • Open, high, close all at same level
  • Long lower wick only
  • No upper wick
📖 Story: Sellers pushed price WAY down — buyers came back and recovered every single point. Complete rejection of lower prices.
❌ Failure: Next candle breaks below the dragonfly low → buyers couldn't hold. Bearish continuation.

◆ Indecision
DOJI
INDECISION
  • Open ≈ Close — tiny or no body
  • Wicks both directions
  • Appears in any trend
📖 Story: Buyers and sellers fought all session — neither side won. The market is undecided. WHO wins the next candle determines direction.
⚠️ Context: In mid-range = noise. At key level after strong trend = significant warning. Volume confirms which one.
SPINNING TOP
INDECISION
  • Small body — any color
  • Long wicks BOTH sides
  • Common in choppy markets
📖 Story: Both sides tried and failed. Buyers pushed up — rejected. Sellers pushed down — rejected. Nobody is in control.
LTPro Rule: MID-RANGE + Spinning Tops = sit on your hands. This is the chop zone. Best trade = no trade.

COIL
  • Fits entirely within prior candle's range

❌ Failure: Breakout in opposite direction of trend = potential reversal forming. Re-evaluate bias immediately.

🚨 THE GOLDEN RULE — From Your Friend

Candles are NOT signals — they are EVIDENCE. You're not predicting. You're reading what already happened: Who's in control? Who just lost? Where did the battle happen? — Structure + Location + Story = Edge.

🧠 THE MENTAL UPGRADE — Order Flow Storytelling

Stop asking "Does this look like a hammer?"
Start asking "Did price REJECT lower prices… or ACCEPT them?"

Rejection = long wick, small body → one side tried and FAILED
Acceptance = large body, no wicks → one side was in complete CONTROL

✋ REJECTION
Long wick = attempt that failed
Small body = indecision or reversal
Wicky candles = battle at that level
Lower wick = buyers rejected sellers
Upper wick = sellers rejected buyers
Examples: Hammer, Shooting Star, Doji, Spinning Top
✅ ACCEPTANCE
Large body = one side in control
No wicks = no pushback at all
Full body candles = momentum
Green Marubozu = accepted higher
Red Marubozu = accepted lower
Examples: Marubozu, strong engulfing, breakout candles
