Pin Bar — Bullish
BULLISH REVERSAL NEW v5
Bulkowski
78%
Order Flow Story
Price sweeps below a key support level (liquidity grab). Bears think they're winning — but institutional buyers step in aggressively, absorbing all sell orders. Price rockets back up. The long lower wick IS the rejection story. Small body near the top = bulls closed in control.
Failure Version
If price closes BELOW the pin bar low on the next candle → pattern failed. Bears overwhelmed. Exit immediately. This is the most common false pin on SPY near FOMC days.
SPY / LTPROv7 Context
Most powerful at VWAP support, prior day lows, or after a liquidity sweep. APEX fires → sweep happens → Pin Bar forms → SIGNAL=BUY → THIS IS THE ENTRY. Wick must be 3:1 or greater to qualify as high-conviction.
Entry Trigger
Buy calls above pin bar high. Stop: below lowest wick + 1 ATR. Delta ≥ 0.60.

Bullish Engulfing
BULLISH REVERSAL NEW v5
Bulkowski
63%
Order Flow Story
Day 1: bears in control, red candle. Day 2: bulls open BELOW Day 1 low (trap), then buy aggressively, driving price above Day 1 open. The green candle CONSUMES the entire prior red candle. Bears capitulated. Smart money buying at discount, retail panic selling = fuel for the move.
Failure Version

SPY / LTPROv7 Context
Most powerful after a multi-candle decline, at demand zones or VWAP. BIAS must flip bullish. Engulfing + VWAP bounce + SIGNAL=BUY = highest-probability long entry of the day.
Entry Trigger
Buy calls at close of engulfing candle. Stop: below engulfing candle low. Delta ≥ 0.60.

Bulkowski
60%
Order Flow Story

SPY / LTPROv7 Context
Watch for Tweezer Tops at prior day highs, round numbers, and VWAP rejection zones. Two equal highs = double-tested supply. SIGNAL=SELL + tweezer at resistance = precision put entry.
Entry Trigger
Buy puts below the low of the second candle. Stop: above both highs + 1 ATR. Delta ≥ 0.60.

Morning Star
BULLISH REVERSAL NEW v5
Bulkowski
72%
Order Flow Story

Candle 3: bulls take over completely, big green candle recovering INTO Candle 1. The star is the turning point — institutional accumulation in the doji/small candle.
Failure Version
If Candle 3 is weak (doesn't recover at least 50% of Candle 1) → bulls didn't show up. Pattern fails. Look for continuation lower on next candle.
SPY / LTPROv7 Context
Powerful after a 2-3 day SPY decline. Watch for it at key support. The doji candle during FOMC = classic Morning Star setup if followed by a rip. SIGNAL=BUY must confirm on Candle 3 close.
Entry Trigger
Buy calls at open of Candle 3 or after Candle 3 closes green. Stop: below Candle 2 low. Delta ≥ 0.60.

Marubozu
CONTINUATION NEW v5
Bulkowski
71%
Order Flow Story
No wicks. Price opened and never looked back. Full ACCEPTANCE of the move — no rejection at either extreme. Green Marubozu: bulls opened and bought all the way to close without hesitation. Red Marubozu: bears opened and sold all the way to close. This is one-sided institutional order flow with zero ambiguity.
Failure Version
⚠️ CRITICAL: A large-bodied RED candle with a small lower wick is NOT a Hammer. A Hammer requires the lower wick to be 2-3x the body AND the body to be small. Marubozu = full body, minimal/no wicks = REJECTION vs ACCEPTANCE framework: this candle means ACCEPTANCE, not rejection.
SPY / LTPROv7 Context
Green Marubozu after FOMC dovish surprise = maximum bullish confirmation. Red Marubozu after hawkish surprise = maximum bearish confirmation. Do NOT fade a Marubozu. Ride it. SIGNAL=BUY on green, SIGNAL=SELL on red. This is the "no doubt" candle.
Entry Trigger
Trade in the direction of the Marubozu. Enter on first pullback or next candle open. Never chase mid-Marubozu — premium explodes. Delta ≥ 0.60.
