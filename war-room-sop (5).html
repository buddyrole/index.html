STEP 1 — DAILY CHART
Daily Targets
DAILY TF
Mark the highest wick and lowest wick of yesterday's candle. These are your ultimate targets for the day. Algorithms magnetically pull price to sweep these extremes before reversing.
BSL Buy-Side Liquidity — Yesterday's highest wick. Calls target. Price sweeps this to grab stops above.
SSL Sell-Side Liquidity — Yesterday's lowest wick. Puts target. Price sweeps this to grab stops below.
NOTE These are wicks, not candle bodies. The wick is where the stop hunt happens.
STEP 2 — 1H CHART
London Fences
1H TF — 03:00–08:00 AM
Mark the highest and lowest wicks from the 03:00 AM to 08:00 AM session. This is the London Range — the global fence. The 9:30 AM open will often fake-out past one of these lines before reversing. That fake-out IS the Judas Swing.
LONG Price sweeps below London Low at open → watch for reversal back up. Judas complete.
SHORT Price sweeps above London High at open → watch for reversal back down. Judas complete.
RULE Do NOT trade the sweep itself. Wait for the reversal candle after the sweep completes.
STEP 3 — 1H CHART
Fair Value Gap
1H TF — IMBALANCE
Find the gap between Candle 1's wick and Candle 3's wick — the middle candle is the impulse move. This gap is unfilled imbalance. Price always wants to return to fill it before continuing.
BULL FVG Price drops into the FVG → expect a bounce UP. The gap acts as a demand magnet.
BEAR FVG Price rallies into the FVG → expect a rejection DOWN. The gap acts as a supply ceiling.
HOW TO C1 high wick to C3 low wick = Bullish FVG zone. C1 low wick to C3 high wick = Bearish FVG zone.
STEP 4 — 1H CHART
Order Block
1H TF — SUPPLY / DEMAND
Find the last opposing candle before a massive move. Mark its full body and wicks. This is where institutional orders were placed. Price returns to this zone to fill remaining orders before continuing the move. Unmitigated OBs are strongest — price has not yet returned.
BULL OB Last RED candle before a massive GREEN move. Demand zone. Price returns here to bounce UP.
BEAR OB Last GREEN candle before a massive RED drop. Supply zone. Price returns here to reject DOWN.
KEY RULE Already-mitigated OBs (price returned once) are significantly weaker. Prioritize fresh unmitigated OBs.
The Daily Wicks are your destination. The London Fences are the trap door. The FVG and Order Block are the launchpad. Price sweeps a London level (Judas) → pulls back into an FVG or OB → you wait for the CBC confirmation → then ride it toward the Daily BSL or SSL. That is the full play from map to target.
