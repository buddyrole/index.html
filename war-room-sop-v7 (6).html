STEP 1 — DAILY CHART
Daily Targets
DAILY TF
Mark the highest wick and lowest wick of yesterday's candle. These are your ultimate targets for the day. Algorithms magnetically pull price to sweep these extremes before reversing.
BSL Buy-Side Liquidity — Yesterday's highest wick. Calls target. Price sweeps this to grab stops above.
SSL Sell-Side Liquidity — Yesterday's lowest wick. Puts target. Price sweeps this to grab stops below.
NOTE These are wicks, not candle bodies. The wick is where the stop hunt happens.
STEP 2 — 1H CHART
London Fences
1H TF — 03:00–08:00 AM
Mark the highest and lowest wicks from the 03:00 AM to 08:00 AM session. This is the London Range — the global fence. The 9:30 AM open will often fake-out past one of these lines before reversing. That fake-out IS the Judas Swing.
LONG Price sweeps below London Low at open → watch for reversal back up. Judas complete.
SHORT Price sweeps above London High at open → watch for reversal back down. Judas complete.
RULE Do NOT trade the sweep itself. Wait for the reversal candle after the sweep completes.
STEP 3 — 1H CHART
Fair Value Gap
1H TF — IMBALANCE
Find the gap between Candle 1's wick and Candle 3's wick — the middle candle is the impulse move. This gap is unfilled imbalance. Price always wants to return to fill it before continuing.
BULL FVG Price drops into the FVG → expect a bounce UP. The gap acts as a demand magnet.
BEAR FVG Price rallies into the FVG → expect a rejection DOWN. The gap acts as a supply ceiling.
HOW TO C1 high wick to C3 low wick = Bullish FVG zone. C1 low wick to C3 high wick = Bearish FVG zone.
STEP 4 — 1H CHART
Order Block
1H TF — SUPPLY / DEMAND
Find the last opposing candle before a massive move. Mark its full body and wicks. This is where institutional orders were placed. Price returns to this zone to fill remaining orders before continuing the move. Unmitigated OBs are strongest — price has not yet returned.
BULL OB Last RED candle before a massive GREEN move. Demand zone. Price returns here to bounce UP.
BEAR OB Last GREEN candle before a massive RED drop. Supply zone. Price returns here to reject DOWN.
KEY RULE Already-mitigated OBs (price returned once) are significantly weaker. Prioritize fresh unmitigated OBs.
The Daily Wicks are your destination. The London Fences are the trap door. The FVG and Order Block are the launchpad. Price sweeps a London level (Judas) → pulls back into an FVG or OB → you wait for the CBC confirmation → then ride it toward the Daily BSL or SSL. That is the full play from map to target.

⭐ CURRENT SPY PATTERN
V Recovery
🟢 BULLISH
Sharp panic selloff followed by equally sharp recovery. Institutions aggressively buy the dip — price snaps back to or above starting point.
ACTION
Buy pullbacks on the right leg. The steeper and faster the V, the more institutional conviction behind it.
⭐ CURRENT SPY PATTERN (Mar→Apr 2026)
Cup & Handle
🟢 BULLISH
Rounded bottom (cup) followed by small pullback (handle) then breakout. Accumulation pattern — takes weeks to months.
ACTION
Buy breakout above cup rim. Handle should be shallow (no more than 50% of cup depth). Higher volume on breakout = confirmation.
Double Bottom (W)
🟢 BULLISH
Two failed attempts to break lower — sellers exhausted. W-shape. Second bottom ideally holds above or at first bottom.
ACTION
Buy breakout above neckline (middle peak). Stop below the second bottom. Target = height of W added to neckline.
Inverse Head & Shoulders
🟢 BULLISH
Three bottoms — middle (head) goes deepest. Major reversal pattern. Takes weeks to form. Very reliable when confirmed.
ACTION
Buy breakout above neckline on high volume. Pullback to neckline after breakout = second entry opportunity.
Ascending Triangle
🟢 BULLISH
Flat resistance top + rising lows = buyers getting more aggressive each time. Pressure builds until breakout.
ACTION
Buy breakout above flat resistance on strong volume. False breakout risk — wait for candle CLOSE above resistance.
Golden Cross
🟢 BULLISH
50-day MA crosses ABOVE 200-day MA. Long-term institutional bull signal. Confirms major trend change from bear to bull.
ACTION
Long-term bull confirmation. Buy pullbacks to 50MA. SPY Golden Cross confirmed July 2025 — still valid.
⭐ CURRENT SPY — Active since Jul 2025
Bear Flag
🔴 BEARISH
Strong move down (flagpole) → tight sideways/up consolidation (flag) → continuation lower. Opposite of bull flag.
ACTION
Buy puts on breakdown below flag support. Target = flagpole length subtracted from breakdown point.
Head & Shoulders
🔴 BEARISH
Three peaks — middle (head) goes highest. Major reversal pattern. Most reliable when volume declines on right shoulder.
ACTION
Buy puts on breakdown below neckline. Watch for pullback to neckline after breakdown = second entry. High reliability pattern.
Double Top (M)
🔴 BEARISH
Two failed attempts to break higher — buyers exhausted. M-shape. Second top ideally at or below first top.
ACTION
Buy puts on breakdown below neckline (middle trough). Stop above the second top. Target = height of M subtracted from neckline.
Death Cross
