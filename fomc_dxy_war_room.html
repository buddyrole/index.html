Stop Loss Shield Rule
🛡️ THE STOP SHIELD
RULE
Place your stop BEYOND the wick tip, not the candle body. During FOMC, market makers WILL sweep wick tips to trigger retail stops before continuing in the intended direction. Give the trade room to breathe by at least 1 ATR beyond the extreme wick.
🟢
Long trade stop: Below the LOWEST wick of the entry candle + 1 ATR buffer
🔴
Short trade stop: Above the HIGHEST wick of the entry candle + 1 ATR buffer
⚠️
FOMC day: Expect stops to be hunted. Widen stops OR reduce size to compensate.
