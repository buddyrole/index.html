How to Use This Tool

STEP 1 — Run your full War Room pre-market drill (Phases 1-3). Check confluence filters above.

STEP 5 — Log the exit price and reason. Track Maverick Filters met to measure confluence impact.

STEP 6 — After 30+ trades check the Analysis tab. Compare your win rate vs the 41% backtest baseline.
