CBC — Candle Body Close

Why the body matters more than the wick

The wick just means price touched the level. The body close means price stayed there — buyers (or sellers) held it for the full candle duration.

Key level = $560
WITHOUT CBC:
Price wicks to $560.50 ← trap
Body closes at $559.20 ← no confirm
You entered and got stopped out
WITH CBC:
Price wicks to $560.50 ← wait
5-min body CLOSES at $560.80 ← confirm
Now you enter with confirmation ✓
Bullish CBCBody closes ABOVE key level = buyers in control = look for calls
Bearish CBCBody closes BELOW key level = sellers in control = look for puts
